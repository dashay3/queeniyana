Love at First Site

Spring Semester 2021

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe After Effects, Audacity

My Final Project I wanted to make two characters falling in love and I did have a hard time at first, I tried to make it exactly how I wanted to be. Artistic inspiration for this project was Love and Disney films. That inspired me to make a Romantic theme for my project. I wanted to make an Animation short for my final. It was about two people becoming lovers at first sight. First I used Adobe Illustrator to make the characters, I wanted them to look like flat paper figures. Then I decide to create a background to make it look like they are in the magical paradise using pinks and reds. Next I imported the file to After Effects to give to create the animation of the two characters holding hands as a big heart appears like a sunrise, and I also animated the small hearts to make them rotate.I did had difficulties on how to use the puppet tool at first and the transform tool but I got use to it by practicing it a little more. Then I used the Audacity software for music. I went to the website FreeSound to find a Romantic theme to the animation. I shortened it to 33 seconds. seconds so it can match the same time as my animation. This has changed my Storyboard a lot because I realized I could not move the characters like 3D characters would look like, so I had to make them move almost like a slight rotation, so I had to make lots of changes to my idea.
